**Allmenningen** is an independent magazine dedicated to covering politics, economics, ecology, and culture from a perspective that puts people and planet first. Founded in 2019, we believe that rigorous journalism and bold ideas are essential tools for building a more democratic, equitable, and sustainable world.

We are not interested in false balance or corporate press releases. We are interested in asking uncomfortable questions, following the money, and amplifying the voices of those building alternatives — from cooperative businesses to community land trusts to public broadband networks.

Our writing is accessible but never dumbed down. We trust our readers to think critically about complex issues, and we provide the context and historical depth that mainstream outlets too often skip.
